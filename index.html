What this platform is

A professional analysis assistant. It reads market data, computes technical indicators, backtests strategies on history, and scores current conditions. It does not place real orders and it never guarantees outcomes.

How to read a Market Scan

Each market gets a score from −100 to +100 built from transparent components: trend (EMA 20/50 confirmed by ADX), momentum (RSI), MACD histogram, stochastic position, and volume. HOT means strong bullish evidence, COLD strong bearish evidence, NEUTRAL no clear edge.

Backtesting honestly

Past performance never guarantees future results. Backtests here include fees, report maximum drawdown alongside returns, and liquidate at the end so strategies are compared on equal terms.

Risk first

Position sizing, stop losses, and exposure limits matter more than entries. Any future live order will always require your explicit confirmation.
