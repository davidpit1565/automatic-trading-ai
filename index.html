What this platform is

A professional analysis assistant. It reads market data, computes technical indicators, backtests strategies on history, and scores current conditions. It does not place real orders and it never guarantees outcomes.

How to read a Market Scan

Each market gets a score from −100 to +100 built from transparent components: trend (EMA 20/50 confirmed by ADX), momentum (RSI), MACD histogram, stochastic position, and volume. HOT means strong bullish evidence, COLD strong bearish evidence, NEUTRAL no clear edge. Click any row to see exactly which indicators drove the score and any warnings.

Backtesting honestly

Past performance never guarantees future results. Backtests here include fees, report maximum drawdown alongside returns, and liquidate at the end so strategies are compared on equal terms. Beware overfitting: a strategy tuned to look great on one history often fails on the next.

Risk first

Position sizing, stop losses, and exposure limits matter more than entries. The roadmap adds a dedicated risk engine before any execution support, and any future live order will always require your explicit confirmation.
